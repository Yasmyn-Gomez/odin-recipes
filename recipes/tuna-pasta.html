Canned Tuna Pasta

For all of the pasta lovers, this tuna pasta recipe is so simple yet so delicious that you will probably be doing it again.

Ingredients:

  • A can of tuna.
  • Any kind of noodles used for pasta.
  • An onion.
  • Olive oil.
  • Garlic cloves.
  • Lemon juice.
  • Salt and pepper.

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta with a bit of salt according to instructions.
  2. While the pasta is boiling, chop the onion and the garlic cloves into brunoise size and reserve.
  3. When the pasta is cooked, reserve some pasta water and drain the rest of it.
  4. In a pan add the oil and when it gets hot, then add the onion and sauté the desired time and add the chopped garlic and sauté again for a few seconds more.
  5. Add the pasta water into the pan and stir.
  6. When the sauce comes to a boil add the tuna and the lemon juice to taste and stir carefully.
  7. Add the pasta and stir carefully as to not break apart the noodles.
  8. Lastly, turn off the heat and serve.
